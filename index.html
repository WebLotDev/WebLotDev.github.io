Basic Package

Our Basic Package is ideal for small businesses or individuals who need a professional online presence without unnecessary complexities.

  • Up to 5 Pages: We create up to 5 custom pages covering the essential needs of your business, such as Home, Services, About Us, Contact, and Additional.
  • Responsive Design: Your website will be fully optimized for any device, including mobile phones and tablets, ensuring an excellent user experience everywhere.
  • Email Support: We provide basic email support for any questions or maintenance needs you may have.

This package offers the perfect balance between functionality and affordability, allowing you to start your online presence with ease and professionalism.

Standard Package

The "Standard" package is ideal for businesses looking to expand their online presence and enhance their visibility in search engines.

  • Up to 10 Pages: We provide up to 10 custom pages to cover your needs, with additional pages available for services, products, news, and anything else your business requires.
  • Responsive Design: Your website will automatically adapt to any device, ensuring an excellent user experience on mobile phones, tablets, and computers.
  • SEO Optimization: We optimize your website for search engines to improve your ranking on Google and attract more traffic.
  • Priority Support: You will receive priority support, ensuring faster responses and solutions to your requests via email or phone.

The "Standard" package is the ideal solution for businesses needing more features and better support to compete in the market.
